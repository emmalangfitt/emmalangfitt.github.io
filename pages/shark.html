shArk

shArk holds a special place in my heart as the first band I started to photograph at Dartmouth. I've become a bit of a groupie and I am positive that their cover of "Best Friend" by Rex Orange County is better than the original. Shooting for this band taught me the difficulties of low lighting in steamy, crowded frat basements. When I reviewed the first set I shot for them, I saw that half the shots were blurry and freaked out, but after plenty of research and practice I've definitely improved on coping with show conditions.
