Lyfted Fest

I had a blast shooting LyftedFest because it showcased so many different bands throughout the day and the crowd was having such a good time. It was nearly a four hour event and I hadn't realized how hazy the room had gotten until I stepped outside and got a breath of actual fresh air. The guitar player in the green baseball shirt was my favorite subject of the day, I thought he glowed on stage but he swears he was just really sweaty.
