Green Key

I got to photograph my friend DJ the opening set for the Green Key concert this past year and I had so much fun running around the big stage trying not to miss a single thing that was happening. The set was about forty minutes long but it felt more like four, I just couldn't believe how quickly it went by. The lights and the fog and the videos meant the background was changing by the minute, and PJ has so much fun onstage that it's hard not to have fun with him.
